About

My name is Isaac Williams, and I'm a writer with a background in technology and data science. In my current role as the Data Visualization Librarian at the University of Texas at Arlington, I teach data science skills to students through in-person workshops and online educational content. Before that, I received an MLIS and a BA (in English) from UCLA. In graduate school, I worked at the UCLA Digital Library Program and the Center for the Study of Political Graphics, where I worked on digital projects and wrote technical documentation. I'm invested in making technology accesible, which led me to become a certified instructor with The Carpentries in 2019.

This website has some examples of my work, including creative and technical writing.
