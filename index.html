About

My name is Isaac Williams, and I'm the Data Visualization Librarian at the University of Texas at Arlington. I teach students and faculty how to use data visualization and digital humanities tools and methods, including Tableau, OpenRefine, Microsoft Excel, and more. I've taught students in a variety of disciplines, from English to Marketing. I'm invested in making digital research accessible to students from non-STEM backgrounds, which is partially what led me to become a certified Instructor with The Carpentries.
